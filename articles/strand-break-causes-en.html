Strand Break During Granulation – Causes and Solutions

Ihor Kryazhev | April 19, 2026

Main Causes of Strand Break

  • Contaminants in the material
  • Incorrect temperature control
  • Clogged screen changer
  • Wrong melt temperature

Solutions

Better material filtration, adjust temperature profiles, clean or replace the screen changer.
